Primary Research Question

How reliable are machine learning models in predicting productivity using behavioral digital data when deployed in real-world environments?

Secondary Research Questions

RQ1
Which behavioral features contribute most significantly to productivity prediction?

RQ2
How does model performance change when behavioral patterns shift over time?

RQ3
How stable are feature importance explanations across different models and retraining cycles?

RQ4
What insights can organizations derive from productivity prediction systems without introducing harmful decision bias?

Hypothesis

H1
Behavioral features such as sleep hours, stress levels, and notification frequency significantly influence productivity predictions.

H2
Tree-based ensemble models such as Random Forest and XGBoost will outperform baseline models in productivity prediction tasks.

H3
Model explanations will reveal that behavioral wellbeing indicators are stronger predictors than raw screen usage time.
